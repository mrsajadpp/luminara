What is Keyword Optimization?

Keyword optimization is the process of improving your pages to better use your target queries to help more people find your website in search engines.

It’s not about finding, analyzing, and choosing keywords. That’s keyword research.

Keyword optimization is about using the right ones in the right places and in the right ways. Which is more likely to help you meet your SEO goals?
